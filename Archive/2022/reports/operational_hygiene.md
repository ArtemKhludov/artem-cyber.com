# 2022: Moving from Construction to Systems Architecture
**Date:** May 2022

## The Pivot
After scaling a construction business to $2M, I realized my real interest lies in the logic of complex systems. IT infrastructure in many companies is as messy as a poorly managed construction site. My goal for AntHouse was to bring operational discipline.

## Initial Actions
- Audited the "black box" server setup.
- Enforced version control for every configuration change.
- Established a "no manual changes" policy to ensure reproducibility.
